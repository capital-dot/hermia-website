1. Purpose

Referrer agrees to refer prospective clients to Company for Company's AI-powered lead intake, qualification, and job-scheduling service ("the Service"), integrated with ServiceM8 and/or Simpro. Company agrees to pay Referrer a referral fee for each referred client who becomes a paying subscriber, as set out below.

2. Referral Fee

For each client referred by Referrer who signs up for and remains a paying, active subscriber of the Service for at least thirty (30) consecutive days, Company will pay Referrer a referral fee equal to % of that client's first full month's subscription fee — regardless of lead channel (Gmail, Outlook, Facebook, or Instagram) or subscription tier.

15% of first month's subscription fee, paid once per successfully referred, retained client.
Example: a client onboarded onto a $260/month plan who stays active 30+ days yields a 15% × $260 = $39 referral payment. A client on a different tier is calculated the same way, off their actual invoiced amount.
  • The fee is a one-time payment per referred client, calculated on that client's actual first month's invoiced subscription amount — it is not recalculated if the client later upgrades or downgrades tier.
  • Payment will be made within fourteen (14) business days of the referred client's 30th consecutive day as an active, paying subscriber.
  • Referral fees apply only to new clients not already in a commercial relationship with Company at the time of referral.
  • No cap on the number of clients referred — the more clients AutoM8 refers, the more they earn, with no minimum volume required.
  • This percentage applies uniformly across all current and future lead channels; no separate per-channel schedule is maintained.

3. Referrer Responsibilities

Referrer agrees to introduce prospective clients in good faith with accurate representation of the Service, provide Company with referred clients' contact details, and not make representations or guarantees on Company's behalf beyond what Company has approved in writing.

4. Company Responsibilities

Company agrees to onboard referred clients promptly, track referrals accurately, and pay referral fees on time as set out in Section 2.

5. Term & Termination

This Agreement begins on the date above and continues until terminated by either Party with fourteen (14) days' written notice. Referral fees earned prior to termination remain payable.

6. Confidentiality

Each Party agrees to keep confidential any non-public business, technical, or client information shared under this Agreement.

7. Co-Marketing / Partner Badge

Company may ask Referrer to provide a "Partner" or "Certified Partner" badge, logo, or similar graphic asset, for optional display on Company's website and marketing materials to indicate this ongoing referral partnership. Providing any such badge is entirely at Referrer's discretion and is not a condition of, or requirement under, this Agreement. Any badge, logo, or trademark supplied remains Referrer's property at all times and is licensed to Company on a non-exclusive, royalty-free, revocable basis solely to indicate the partnership. Company will remove it promptly upon termination of this Agreement or at Referrer's written request at any time.

8. Independent Parties

Referrer is an independent party and not an employee, partner, or agent of Company. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship.
