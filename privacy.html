1. Global Data Sovereignty & Stewardship

Hermia ("the Platform" ) operates as a high-precision data processing layer for the real estate industry. We are committed to the highest standards of data integrity, complying with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 (PDPL).

Zero-Access Architecture

Unlike "Tier 2" legacy systems, Hermia utilizes Zero-Access Architecture. We utilize bank-grade OAuth 2.0 protocols to sync with your CRM and Inbox. This means we never see, store, or have access to your raw account passwords.

2. Data Collection & Classification

We process information categorized into three distinct tiers:

  • Tier A (Identity Data): Business credentials and contact details required for enterprise billing and account management.
  • Tier B (Lead Intelligence): Real-time enquiry data (Name, Phone, Requirements) captured from authorized portals (REA, Domain, Bayut, Property Finder).
  • Tier C (Conversational Metadata): Structured qualification logs generated during AI-driven WhatsApp and SMS engagements.

3. Advanced Processing & Security

Hermia is built on Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure (Make.com Level 3 Architect Certified). Data is processed in secure, volatile memory and is only retained as long as necessary to facilitate the "Hot Lead" push to your CRM. We employ 256-bit AES encryption for all data in transit.

4. International Data Transfers

As a global platform serving Australia and the UAE, data may be processed across secure sovereign borders. We ensure all third-party sub-processors (including OpenAI, Unipile, and Stripe) maintain rigorous SOC2 or equivalent compliance standards.
