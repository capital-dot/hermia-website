Privacy Policy

Effective 18 May 2026 • ABN 75 434 177 026

1. Who We Are

Hermia ("we", "us", "our") is an Australian sole trader (ABN 75 434 177 026) providing SMS and WhatsApp lead qualification software for real estate agents. Our website is hermia.au. Contact: capital@hermia.au

2. What Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Agent clients: Business name, contact name, email address, phone number, CRM details, and billing information provided during onboarding.
  • End leads (property enquirers): Name, phone number, and enquiry data passed to Hermia by the agent via their CRM or listing platform.
  • Automated interactions: SMS and WhatsApp message content generated through our qualification flows.
  • Usage data: System logs, scenario activity, and error reports from our Make.com automation platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and operate the Hermia lead qualification service.
  • To route qualified leads back to agents via their nominated CRM.
  • To process payments via Stripe.
  • To communicate with agents regarding their account and service updates.
  • To improve our automation flows and product reliability.

4. Third-Party Services

Hermia integrates with the following third-party platforms to deliver its service. Each platform operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Make.com — automation infrastructure
  • ClickSend — SMS delivery
  • Unipile — WhatsApp and messaging integration
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • HubSpot, Zoho, Airtable, Rex Software — CRM integrations (where enabled by agent)
  • OpenAI — AI language processing

Hermia is independent software and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially partnered with any of the above platforms. All trademarks belong to their respective owners.

5. Australian Privacy Act Compliance

We handle personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This includes:

  • Only collecting information that is necessary for our service.
  • Not disclosing personal information to third parties except as required to deliver the service or comply with law.
  • Taking reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, or unauthorised access.
  • Allowing individuals to access and correct their personal information upon request.

6. Data Storage and Security

Lead data is stored in secure cloud-based data stores (Make.com Data Store). Billing data is handled entirely by Stripe and is not stored on Hermia systems. We retain lead interaction data only as long as necessary to deliver the service to the agent.

7. SMS and WhatsApp Consent

Hermia sends automated SMS and WhatsApp messages to property enquirers on behalf of real estate agents. Agents are responsible for ensuring they have obtained appropriate consent from leads in accordance with the Spam Act 2003 (Cth) and applicable telecommunications regulations. Hermia processes these messages as a service provider acting on the agent's instructions.

8. International Transfers

Some third-party services we use may process data outside Australia, including in the United States and UAE. We take reasonable steps to ensure these providers maintain appropriate data protection standards.
