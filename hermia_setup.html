Important · Tell Your Customers

Tell customers to check WhatsApp

Hermia qualifies leads via WhatsApp. Once a customer submits an enquiry, Hermia follows up there — so it's worth letting them know in advance to keep an eye on it.

Tip: Add a line to your booking confirmation or a section on your website stating that they will receive a WhatsApp message—'Please check WhatsApp; our team will follow up there shortly'—so leads don't slip through the cracks unanswered.
