What Agents Actually Ask Us

The questions we get
on every demo call

These are the real concerns — answered plainly, without any technical spin.

🤔

Will my competitor see my leads?

Never. Every agent's pipeline is completely isolated. Your leads are only visible to you and your team — nobody else on the platform can see them, access them, or know they exist.
🤔

Will this break at the worst possible time?

Hermia is built by a Make Certified Level 3 Professional — the same automation infrastructure used by enterprise companies at scale. The system includes redundant error-handling so no lead is ever dropped, even if a step fails. It's designed to be invisible and reliable.
🤔

Who else has access to my buyer data?

Nobody. Buyer enquiry data is captured, processed, and pushed directly to your CRM. It is never stored in a shared database, never handed to a third party, and never retained externally once the qualification session closes.
🤔

Is this legal in Australia and the UAE?

Yes. Hermia only contacts buyers who initiated contact through your listings — that's the consent trigger under the Australian Spam Act 2003. Every message identifies your agency and includes a STOP/unsubscribe option. All operations are within Australian Privacy Act 1988 and UAE data protection boundaries. Documentation is available on request.
🤔

Is Hermia pretending to be me when it messages buyers?

No. Hermia introduces itself as your digital assistant — not you. Every buyer knows they're talking to an automated qualification system. This is both transparent and legally compliant. Your personal identity and tone are never impersonated.
