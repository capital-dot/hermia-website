The Process

How we operate.

No dashboards to learn, no admin to manage. Here's exactly what happens between an enquiry landing and a job hitting your Simpro or AroFlo calendar.

From lead arrival to job dispatch, Hermia handles every step—qualification, scheduling, and data integrity—without you lifting a finger.

01

The lead comes in.

An enquiry lands in Gmail or Outlook. Hermia reads it the moment it arrives and starts qualifying — scope, site details, urgency, and who's accountable for the bill.

02

Hermia asks permission first.

Before the conversation moves anywhere, Hermia emails the client asking for their consent to continue the conversation — in line with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles. No client is ever moved to a new channel without their explicit sign-off.

03

Consent given — Hermia takes the conversation from here.

Once the client agrees, Hermia continues the conversation directly on Hermia's own dedicated number — not yours. Your team never sees a raw chat thread and never has to manage a conversation. You get the finished result: a clean, structured, ready-to-book job in Simpro or AroFlo.

04

Aggressive qualification — no vague answers, ever.

Hermia doesn't accept incomplete information. It keeps working the conversation until every question you've configured is actually answered — following up automatically if a lead skips something you've marked as required. No guessing, no gaps, nothing for your office to chase afterward.

05

Smart scheduling — matched to available staff.

Hermia doesn't just book a job blindly. Once the lead gives their availability, Hermia checks your technician roster for who's actually free that day — and looks up to five days ahead if nobody is. The lead is told exactly which technician is assigned and on what date, before the conversation even ends.

06

Job booked — reports delivered.

Once fully qualified and scheduled, the job lands in your Simpro or AroFlo account with a technician assigned, ready to dispatch. Every week, a business performance report lands in your inbox summarising everything that came through.

Data Integrity

We protect your CRM data.

Hermia never blindly creates a new customer and site for every enquiry. Before anything is booked, it checks what already exists in your Simpro or AroFlo build — and only creates what's actually missing.

Scenario 01

Existing customer, existing site

Hermia matches the enquiry straight to the customer and site already on file — no new records created at all.

Result: qualified job booked directly against your existing records. Zero duplicates.
Scenario 02

Existing customer, new site

Hermia recognises the customer but sees this is a site they haven't used before — so it creates only the new site, and reuses the existing customer ID.

Result: one new site record. Customer record stays exactly as it was.
Scenario 03

Brand new customer

No match found anywhere. Hermia creates a new customer ID and a new site, fully populated with everything gathered from the enquiry.

Result: a clean new record, booked and ready — nothing for your office to tidy up.
Pricing & Custom Fields

Pricing, estimated automatically.

Most contractors lose hours chasing leads back and forth over price. Hermia removes that step entirely — using pricing criteria you set during onboarding, not a guess.

When a lead describes their situation, Hermia matches that description against your pricing tiers and gives them a real estimate — for example, roughly what a burst pipe repair costs based on your own rates. From there, it's their call.

If they accept

The estimate is logged straight into the job record in Simpro or AroFlo, noting the figure they agreed to — so there's no confusion about price when the job lands on your schedule.

If they negotiate

Hermia instantly hands them the negotiation contact you set during onboarding — your phone or your email, whichever you chose. You're told they're reaching out about pricing before they ever contact you, and the job still lands in your CRM either way.

Your custom fields, mapped automatically.

Hermia doesn't just fill in the default fields. During setup, you tell us the custom fields your business actually uses — and what each one is for — and those become the exact questions Hermia asks every lead before the job goes ahead. Every qualified detail is mapped straight into the correct custom field, exactly where your team expects to find it. Hermia doesn't change your CRM, your workflow, or your naming conventions — it simply learns them and works inside them.

Want to change what's asked? Just email us. Updates to your question set are usually live within 1–3 business days — no new onboarding call required.

The Real Issues

Problems we actually solve.

Beyond messy CRM data and pricing friction, contractors face a gauntlet of daily operational headaches. Here's how Hermia tackles them head-on.

👻

Ghost Leads & Long ETAs

The Problem: Customers go radio silent after submitting a form. Office wastes time booking vague "9 AM to 1 PM" windows, leading to frustrated clients.

Hermia's Solution: Instant engagement skyrockets response rates. Hermia captures exact availability and confirms a specific slot directly in the conversation, locking clients in before they jump to competitors.

🚨

Emergency Callout Chaos

The Problem: 11 PM panic call: "There's water everywhere!" Tech rolls out blind, finds a minor fixture leak, or lacks the part needed.

Hermia's Solution: Hermia forces categorization — "send a photo or short video here." Media and a clear description drop straight into the job notes, letting techs triage, check inventory, or reject the midnight call.

😴

Out-of-Hours Burnout

The Problem: Owners glued to phones 24/7. Miss an 8 PM email by 15 minutes? Client goes to the next Google listing. Constant burnout.

Hermia's Solution: Hermia acts as your 24/7 advanced receptionist. Greeting, vetting, pricing, booking — all autonomous. The job sits clean in your CRM by sunrise while you sleep.

🔧

Poor First-Time Fix Rates

The Problem: Tech arrives with incomplete notes from a frantic phone call. Spends 30 minutes diagnosing, realizes they don't have materials, reschedules. Profitability dies.

Hermia's Solution: Hermia collects deep, standardized technical details using the exact criteria you configured. The tech opens the job and sees structured data with photos and video — not a vague note.

📱

Tech Adoption Friction

The Problem: Field service software is powerful but has a steep learning curve. Field staff hate navigating clunky interfaces, leading to missing information.

Hermia's Solution: Data collection happens entirely on the client's side, in conversation — never inside a clunky app. By the time the tech opens the job, it's already perfectly laid out. No complex menus. Just work.
