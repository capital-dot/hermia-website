The Process

How we operate.

No dashboards to learn, no admin to manage. Here's exactly what happens between an enquiry landing and a qualified, ready-to-book job hitting your CRM.

01

The lead comes in.

An enquiry lands in Gmail or Outlook — direct, forwarded, or inside a platform email. Hermia reads it the moment it arrives, finds the real person who needs help, and starts qualifying: scope, site, urgency, and who's accountable for the bill.

02

Hermia asks permission first.

Before the conversation moves anywhere, Hermia emails the client for consent to continue. No client is ever moved to a new channel without explicit sign-off — in line with the privacy and consent laws that apply in your market.

03

Consent given — Hermia takes the conversation.

Qualification continues on Hermia's own dedicated, verified number — not yours. Your team never sees a raw chat thread and never manages a conversation. You get the finished result: a clean, structured, ready-to-book job.

04

Aggressive qualification — no vague answers, ever.

Hermia doesn't accept incomplete information. It keeps working the conversation until every question you've configured is actually answered — naming exactly what's missing, merging follow-ups with what was already said, and filtering spam and tire kickers without a human seeing them. A gas smell escalates instantly; active flooding is flagged as an emergency on the spot.

05

Booked on Simpro — or a warm lead in your pocket on AroFlo.

With Simpro, when the lead names their availability, Hermia pulls your actual staff list and schedule from your CRM, scans the next 20 days, works out which technician is genuinely free at the time asked for, and books the closest match. The job is created, the technician assigned, and the appointment lands on your calendar as a real scheduled activity — exactly as if your best office admin had checked the whiteboard and pencilled it in. The lead is told which technician is coming, and when, before the conversation ends.

With AroFlo, Hermia doesn't touch your calendar. Instead it fills in your client and location records, then hands you the qualified lead directly on WhatsApp — rated warm or hot, never cold noise — and you book it in when you're ready. Either way, cold enquiries are filtered out before they ever reach you.

06

Job delivered — reports every Monday.

The job lands fully briefed: problem, exact location, severity, history, photos, access instructions, on-site contact, agreed estimate, booked slot (on Simpro), assigned technician, priority — a work order your tech can load the ute from. Every Monday, a business summary lands in your inbox with the total emergencies, urgent jobs and standard jobs that came through.
