How pricing

Quick overview before you set anything up.

One list, every quote

Give us your prices once. Hermia uses that same list to quote every lead who messages in — no manual work per lead.

How the number gets picked

When a lead describes their situation, Hermia matches it against your price list and works out the closest fit — always an estimate, never a final price.

Format it clearly

List each service with its price, e.g. "Callout fee — $120." The clearer your list, the more accurate the match.

If they want to negotiate

Leads can accept the estimate or ask to negotiate — if they do, Hermia passes them the contact detail you set on the next step.

Update anytime

Change your prices or contact detail whenever you like — new quotes always use your latest version.
