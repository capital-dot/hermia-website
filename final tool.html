The Five Pillars

The data we extract before you call

Every enquiry that replies to Hermia surfaces five structured data points into your CRM — so you know exactly who to call first.

I.
Budget
What range can they actually afford — captured in price tier bands so you know immediately if they match your listings.
II.
Timeline
Moving in 4 weeks vs 6 months — the single most important signal separating a hot lead from a cold one.
III.
Finance Status
Pre-approved, pending, or cash buyer — captured so you know if the deal can actually close before you invest your time.
IV.
Intent
Buying, selling, renting, or investing — different scoring logic applies to each, so priority is always accurate.
V.
Enquiry
The specific listing question they asked, captured verbatim and attached to the lead profile in your CRM.
