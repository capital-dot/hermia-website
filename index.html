How It Works

One enquiry becomes a
ranked, ready-to-call lead
in under 90 seconds.

Hermia sits between your portal and your CRM. It does the interviewing, the ranking, and the data entry — so you only ever do the closing.

1

Buyer enquires from REA, Domain, or Property Finder

The instant a buyer taps "Contact Agent", Hermia captures the lead data from the webhook — before you've even been notified. No manual check-in required.

Instant portal integration
2

Hermia sends 3 qualification questions via WhatsApp & SMS

The buyer gets a message from your digital assistant — not you pretending to be online at 11pm. Hermia introduces itself and asks three short, structured questions. Timeline. Budget. Finance flexibility. The answers are letter-choices (A/B/C/D) — fast to reply, clean to process.

Structured qualification workflow
3

Inspection requests are handled automatically

If the buyer wants to view a property, Hermia checks your listed inspection times and books them in. If you haven't listed times yet, Hermia sends you the only email you'll ever get from it: "This buyer wants to view. Add times." That's it. No other notifications.

Zero back-and-forth scheduling
4

Every lead is scored Hot, Warm, or Cold — based on logic, not AI guesswork

Hermia's scoring is fully condition-based — every path is predetermined. Pre-approved and moving within 4 weeks? Hot. 1–3 month window? Warm. Early research? Cold. No hallucinations. No surprises. Just consistent, auditable logic that ranks every lead the same way, every time.

Deterministic scoring — not AI chat
5

Your CRM is pre-filled before you open your laptop

Name, number, budget, timeline, finance status, property interest, lead score — all silently synced into your CRM. No digest emails. No "hot lead" pings. You open your CRM at 8am and the pipeline is already built. Start calling Hot leads immediately.

Zero inbox noise · Pure pipeline

Why Hermia is not a chatbot — and why that matters

AI chatbots improvise answers, misread context, and hallucinate. Hermia doesn't. Every question is scripted. Every valid answer is a letter choice. Every outcome is pre-defined by conditions. Once the 3 questions are complete, the session closes permanently — the buyer's record is deleted. Any further messages from that number get no response. The system is sealed. No open-ended conversation, no data drift, no unpredictable outputs. Just clean, structured qualification data in your CRM.

✉️

The only email you'll ever get from Hermia

When a buyer wants to inspect a property and you haven't listed inspection times on the portal, Hermia emails you once: "This buyer wants to view 15 Palm Jumeirah Drive. Add inspection times so they can book." That's it. No daily digests. No "new lead" pings. No notification for every Hot, Warm, or Cold lead — those go silently to your CRM. Just the one email that actually needs your action.
