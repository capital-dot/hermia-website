How It Works

Simple. Automatic.
Always running.

Watch the full walkthrough, then see exactly how Hermia fits into your existing workflow without changing anything about how you advertise.

1

A buyer enquires on your listing

Via Domain, Realestate.com.au, or your website. You do not change anything about how you advertise or where your listings sit. Hermia connects to your existing enquiry flow.

Your leads. Your listings.
2

Hermia responds via SMS within 30 seconds

The buyer receives a professional reply the moment their enquiry lands. It acknowledges their interest and opens the qualification conversation immediately — while the lead is still warm.

Under 30 seconds. Every time.
3

Five qualification questions asked automatically

Budget range. Buying timeline. Finance and pre-approval status. Buying intent. Specific property requirements. Every lead goes through the same consistent process. Hermia does not answer real estate questions — it collects information only and summarises it for you.

Consistent. Structured. Every lead.
4

Lead scored Hot, Warm or Cold

Based on their responses, Hermia assigns a priority score. Pre-approved and moving this month — Hot. Actively searching in a 1–3 month window — Warm. Early research with no finance — Cold.

Clear priority. No guessing.
5

Full summary pushed directly into your Zoho CRM

Name, number, budget, timeline, finance status, requirements, and score — all pre-filled and waiting. You open your CRM, see who is Hot, check your stock, make one call. Commission earned.
