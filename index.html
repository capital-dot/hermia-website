The real problem
Contractors don’t lose massive commercial jobs
to better field techs.

They lose them to more organised offices. Property managers and facilities teams blast the exact same defect reports and service requests to five or six companies at the exact same time. The contractor who responds first with a clear job scope, verified site details, and a flawlessly professional Simpro brief wins the contract — not the cheapest, not the quickest. The most prepared.
