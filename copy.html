Step 3 of 3
Paste into Gmail settings
Go back to Gmail and follow these steps to finish activating forwarding.
  • 1
    Go back to Gmail
  • 2
    Click the gear icon (top right)
  • 3
    Click See all settings
  • 4
    Click the Forwarding and POP/IMAP tab
  • 5
    Hit Refresh on that page
  • 6
    You'll see "Forward a copy of incoming mail to" — paste your alias into that box, then select "keep Gmail's copy in the inbox"
  • 7
    Scroll down and click Save Changes
What it should look like
Forward a copy of incoming mail to your-alias and keep Gmail's copy in the inbox
